How To Buy

01

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in the form of a browser extension) and TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add Bitrock to your network-list.

02

Buy and send Bitrock to MetaMask

Buy Bitrock on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address.

03

Head on to Rockswap

Head on over to Rockswap and use the Contract Address to find the Token.

04

View and HODL

Swap Bitrock for the Token. Now you need to add it to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet to view your current holdings. Lastly, HODL!
